Practical Coping Tips

Immediate techniques

  • 5-4-3-2-1 grounding: Name 5 things you can see, 4 you can touch, 3 you can hear, 2 you can smell, 1 you can taste — slow and steady.
  • Box breathing: Inhale 4s — hold 4s — exhale 4s — hold 4s. Repeat 4 cycles.
  • Slow belly breaths: Breathe in for 4, out for 6 — focus on the belly rising and falling.

Daily habits that help

  • Sleep routine: Aim for consistent sleep/wake times and a wind-down ritual (limit screens 30–60 mins before bed).
  • Move regularly: Short walks, stretching, or gentle yoga — 10–30 minutes most days improves mood.
  • Eat regularly: Small balanced meals and hydration help energy and concentration.
